An Interview: Stephanie Andolsek

what is she thinking?

November 9th, 2019
WHY DO YOU WANT TO DESIGN?

I want to bring together elements and principles to make something look beautiful, abstract and/or unusual.

WHAT IS DIFFICULT IN DESIGN AND HOW DO YOU WORK TO PUSH PAST THAT?

Using the software to create my designs especially coming from a background with no experience with the software. I push past my frustrations by watching tutorials, tutorials, tutorials & asking for help.

WHAT DO YOU SEE YOURSELF DOING AFTER THIS PROGRAM?

Going to Germany with my brother and completing a degree within the design world. Im keeping my options open.

WHAT EXCITES YOU ABOUT THIS PROGRAM?

The result of putting all of the elements together in a cohesive way and appreciating what I see.

WHAT WORRIES ABOUT YOU ABOUT DESIGN?

That Im never good enough.

CAN DESIGN CHANGE THE WORLD?

Yes, when used responsibly design can change the world. Design I feel, draws attention to issues such as reducing plastic waste.

WHAT IS A WEBSITE?

Its a platform to share your ideas.

WHAT MAKES A GOOD DESIGNER?

I feel as long as the designer is happy with what they are creating designs will come out.

WHAT SKILLS SHOULD A GREAT DESIGNER HAVE?

knowing and understanding form, perspective, principles and elements of design as well as other perspectives from established designers.

WHAT ARE YOUR STRENGTHS & WEAKNESSES?

My strengths are persistence, perserverance, being 40 with no design background. My Weaknesses are: the challenge I have to bring my ideas to life when using the software.
